Overview

Profile Analyzer instantly evaluates any public LinkedIn or Upwork profile and generates a comprehensive, unbiased report right in your browser. Whether you’re a recruiter, hiring manager, or career coach, streamline candidate screening with metrics on experience, skills, personality, red flags, and suggested next-level roles. One click delivers a clear verdict: “Hire,” “Maybe,” or “No-hire.”

Key Features

  • Instant Experience Snapshot

    Calculates total years of full-time experience and number of distinct roles. Displays a concise “Snapshot” card for immediate career overview.

  • Automated Skills Grouping

    Detects every on-profile skill and organizes them into categories (e.g. Front-end, Back-end, Cloud & Deployment). Includes an interactive pie chart and searchable tag cloud.

  • Personality X-Ray

    Scores Risk Taker, Innovator, and Collaborator (1–5) with evidence snippets like “Built AI integrations” or “Led cross-functional teams.”

  • Next-Level Career Fit

    Suggests plausible upward transitions (e.g. Technical Architect, Product Manager) based on experience, projects, and personality.

  • Red Flags & Credibility Concerns

    Flags inconsistent tenures, buzzword-heavy summaries, missing impact metrics, and more in a simple bullet list.

  • Final Verdict

    Combines all insights into one actionable recommendation: “Hire,” “Maybe,” or “No-hire,” helping you prioritize fast.

  • One-Click Export

    Download full JSON reports instantly. Pro users get watermark-free PDF summaries and CSV skill exports.

  • Free & Paid Plans

    Free tier: one analysis every 12 hours. Pro tier: unlimited analyses, richer insights, and monthly updates.

How It Works

Step 1: Install & Activate

Add Profile Analyzer from the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons. Free users get one analysis every 12 hours; upgrade to Pro for unlimited real-time reports.

Step 2: Navigate to Profile

Open any public LinkedIn or Upwork profile you want to evaluate.

Step 3: Set Your “Target Role”

Enter the job title you’re hiring for (e.g. “Lead Full-Stack Developer,” “Marketing Director”) to contextualize the analysis.

Step 4: Click “Analyze”

Click the extension icon and hit “Analyze.” Within 30–60 seconds, view a full dashboard of experience, skills, personality scores, red flags, next-level roles, and a final verdict.

Step 5: Review & Export

Download a JSON report instantly. Pro users can also generate PDF or CSV exports to compare multiple candidates side-by-side.
