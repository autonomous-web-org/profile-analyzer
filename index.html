Overview

Profile Analyzer instantly evaluates any public LinkedIn or Upwork profile using advanced algorithms; one click delivers a clear verdict: “Hire”, “Maybe”, or “No-hire”.

How It Works

Step 1: Install & Activate

Add Profile Analyzer from the Chrome Web Store. Enjoy one free analysis every 12 hours; upgrade to Pro for unlimited real-time reports.

Step 2: Navigate to Profile

Open any public LinkedIn or Upwork profile you want to evaluate.

Step 3: Set Your “Target Role”

Enter the job title you’re hiring for, for example “Software Engineer” or “Marketing Director”, to contextualize the analysis.

Step 4: Click “Analyze”

Click the extension icon and hit “Analyze.” Within seconds, view skills gap, personality scores, red flags, next-level roles, and a final verdict.

Step 5: Review & Decide

Review the dashboard insights and make informed hiring decisions quickly.

Key Features

Instant Experience Snapshot

Calculates total years of full-time experience and recent role history; displays a concise “Snapshot” card for an immediate career overview.

Automated Skills Grouping

Detects and categorizes on-profile skills into Core, Soft, and Technical buckets; includes an interactive pie chart and tag cloud.

Personality X-Ray

Scores Risk Taker, Innovator, and Collaborator by analyzing profile content; highlights key personality traits.

Custom Role Context

Tailors analysis by specifying your target role; adjusts scoring to match the desired position.

Comprehensive Dashboard

View summaries of functionality, next-level fit, red flags, and verdict at a glance.

Free vs. Pro Plans

Free plan allows one analysis every 12 hours; Pro unlocks unlimited analyses, richer insights, and monthly updates.
