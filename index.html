Overview

Profile Analyzer instantly evaluates any public LinkedIn or Upwork profile and generates a comprehensive, unbiased report right in your browser. Whether you’re a recruiter, hiring manager, or career coach, streamline candidate screening with metrics on experience, skills, personality, red flags, and suggested next-level roles. One click delivers a clear verdict: “Hire”, “Maybe”, or “No-hire”.

How It Works

Step 1: Install & Activate

Add Profile Analyzer from the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons. Free users get one analysis every 12 hours; upgrade to Pro for unlimited real-time reports.

Step 2: Navigate to Profile

Open any public LinkedIn or Upwork profile you want to evaluate.

Step 3: Set Your "Target Role"

Enter the job title you’re hiring for, for example, “Lead Full-Stack Developer” or “Marketing Director”, to contextualize the analysis.

Step 4: Click "Analyze"

Click the extension icon and hit "Analyze". Within 30 to 60 seconds, view a full dashboard of experience, skills, personality scores, red flags, next-level roles, and a final verdict.

Step 5: Review & Decide

Review the dashboard insights and make informed hiring decisions quickly.

Key Features

  • Instant Experience Snapshot

    Calculates total years of full-time experience and number of distinct roles, and displays a concise "Snapshot" card for an immediate career overview.

  • Automated Skills Grouping

    Detects and categorizes on-profile skills, such as Front-end, Back-end, and Cloud, and includes an interactive pie chart and tag cloud.

  • Personality X-Ray

    Scores Risk Taker, Innovator, and Collaborator on a scale of 1 to 5, with evidence snippets like "Built AI integrations" or "Led cross-functional teams".

  • Next-Level Career Fit

    Suggests upward roles such as Technical Architect or Product Manager based on experience and personality.

  • Red Flags & Credibility Concerns

    Flags inconsistent tenures, buzzword-heavy summaries, and missing impact metrics in a simple bullet list.

  • Final Verdict

    Combines all insights into a single recommendation: “Hire”, “Maybe”, or “No-hire”.

  • Free & Paid Plans

    Free plan allows one analysis every 12 hours. Pro plan offers unlimited analyses, richer insights, and monthly updates.
